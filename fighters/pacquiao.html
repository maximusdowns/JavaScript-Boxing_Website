Manny Pacquiao

Manny Pacquiao truly embodies the story of the modern fighter. Born unto poverty on December 17th, 1978 in Kivawe, Bukidnon, Philippines, his life has been an uphill struggle.

He moved to Mianila at the age of 14. He began his time there living on the streets, taking up boxing as a means of good. His first fights were for rice to feed his four other siblings

He made the Philippine national ameateur boxing team, which covered his room and board. He amassed a record of 60-4 as an amateur.

He moved to the United States to start his career as a professional. Comin under the training of former fighter Freddie Roach, he has since become the only fighter to win titles in eight different weight classes.
