Floyd Mayweather

Considered to be a once in a generation fighter, Floyd Mayweather is considered pound-for-pound the best boxer in the world.

Mayweather was born in Grand Rapids, Michigan on February 24, 1977. Both his father, Floyd Mayweather Sr., and his uncle, Rodger Mayweather, were professional boxers. Floyd never took any other profession seriously as a potential.

Floyd fought a total of 90 times an an amatuer, gaining a record of 84-6. H won a bronze medal at the olympic games, representing the United States of America
