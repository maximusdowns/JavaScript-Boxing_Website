The Story

Well folks, after years and years of missed opportunities, failed negotiations, and excuses, two of the best fighters in the world are going to square off for pound for pound supremacy.

This comes, of course, after Manny Pacquiao has managed to revitalize his career after the devesating knockout loss to long time rival Juan Manuel Marquez in the fourth fight of their storied rivalry.

Pacquiao has, of course, rebounded with wins over American banger Brandon Rios and avenging his questionable loss to technician Timothy Bradley.

Floyd "Money" Mayweather remains unbeaten in his hall of fame career. After putting on a masterpiece performance against rising national icon Saul "Canelo" Alvarez, Floyd seem to miss a step or two against hard hittng Argentine Marcos Maidana.

Despite pulling out the unanimous decision, many wondering if Floyd has lost a step or two from his otherwise impervious defense.

The world now watches and waits to see what the result of these two lions final meeting in the squared circle will be. It will all be decided in the ring.
